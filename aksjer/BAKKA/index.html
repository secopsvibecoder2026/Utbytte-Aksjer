Om selskapet

P/F Bakkafrost, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the production and sale of salmon products in North America, Western Europe, Eastern Europe, Asia, and internationally. The company operates through seven segments: Fishmeal, Fish Oil and Fish Feed; Freshwater Faroe Islands; Freshwater Scotland; Farming Faroe Islands; Farming Scotland; Services; and Sales & Other.
