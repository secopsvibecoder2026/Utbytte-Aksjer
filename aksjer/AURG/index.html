Om selskapet

Aurskog Sparebank provides various banking products and services for individuals and corporate customers in Norway. The company offers business, placement, depositumskonto, agricultural, and customer accounts, as well as accounts systems. It also provides green business, bank guarantee, business, construction, factoring, and cash credit loans, as well as leasing services. In addition, it offers payment solutions, such as direct debit, corporate connect, einvoice, in-store cash services, payment terminal, and Vipps; cards and payment solutions.
