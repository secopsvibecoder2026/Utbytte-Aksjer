Om selskapet

Byggma ASA, together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and supplies building materials in Norway, the United Kingdom, Sweden, Finland, Denmark, and internationally. It operates through Panels, Beams, Windows, and Lighting segments. The company offers pre-painted ceilings and wall panels under the Huntonit brand; I-beams for use in ceilings, walls, and floors for wood-based buildings under the Masonite brand; and particle boards, boards, and structural panels for use in buildings, interior ceilings, and wall panels under the Forestia brand.
